Frequently Asked Questions

All you need to know before booking with us

How can I book a room? ▶

You can easily book a room by selecting your dates and number of guests in the booking form and clicking "Book Now".

Can I cancel my booking? ▶

Yes, you can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your arrival without any extra charges.

Are there group discounts available? ▶

Yes, we offer special discounts for large groups. Please contact us for more details.

What are the check-in and check-out times? ▶

Check-in starts at 2:00 PM and check-out is until 12:00 PM.

Are pets allowed? ▶

Sorry, pets are not allowed in the hotel.
